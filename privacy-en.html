Privacy Policy

Readiness · Effective date: [EFFECTIVE DATE]

Readiness is built privacy-first. In plain terms: your health data stays on your iPhone. This policy explains exactly what the app does with your data.

1. What the app reads

With your permission, Readiness reads the following from Apple Health (HealthKit):

  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Sleep
  • Active energy / activity

It uses these to calculate your daily readiness score and to write on-device insights.

2. Where your data goes — nowhere

  • All processing happens on your device. Your health data is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. There is no server, no account, and no login.
  • Scores, day logs, and settings are stored locally on your device (and in your private iCloud device backup if you have that enabled — controlled by Apple, not us).
  • We use no analytics or advertising SDKs. We do not track you and we collect no personal data.

3. AI insights

Insights are generated on your device. On iPhone 15 Pro and newer with Apple Intelligence enabled, Apple's on-device model may write them — this also runs locally and is not used to send your data to us. For everyone else, insights come from a built-in, on-device generator.

4. Notifications

If you turn on the morning reminder, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not run a server and send no push notifications.

5. Purchases

Readiness Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

6. Children

Readiness is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country) and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Deleting your data

Because everything is stored on your device, deleting the app removes your Readiness data from that device. Your Apple Health data itself stays in the Health app and is managed by you there.

8. Changes to this policy

If a future version sends any data off your device (for example, an optional cloud AI feature), we will update this policy and the App Store privacy label before that feature ships, and describe it clearly.
